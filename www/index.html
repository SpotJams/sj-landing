HUMANS TOGETHER FOR MUSIC

SpotJams is a Community that brings humans together for music. Create tracks and share them with friends or the world. Discover new muscians by location, instrument or genre. Connect and then Collaborate over the internet with track layering or meet up in real life to play music together. Grow as a musician and Expand your community.
